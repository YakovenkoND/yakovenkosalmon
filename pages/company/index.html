The Company

Salmonbellies AS understands that sales and marketing of fish products is a people to people business often in a multicultural work environment globally, and that is exactly why we value employees with a higher education to meet the criterias as in several other kinds of operations, are continuously becoming increasingly more complex to lead, manage and control. Our company is motivated and able to help build market trends by simply guide our clients and supply the market on a weekly, monthly and bi-monthly basis. Salmonbellies practices work ethics when consulting operations of aquaculture farms and/or when selling trout and salmon products to the world markets.

Operations

All fish species raised belong to the Salmonidae family, and these species are collectively referred to as salmonids with 32 production site facilities under our partnership venture umbrella: The sites are located in Finland, Aaland and Sweden

Main products: fresh gutted rainbow trout, sea trout and rainbow trout roe

Annual production: 10 million kilos

Current processing combined annually is 20 000 ton, however we are scheduled to double processing to 40 000 ton in the year 2020.
